Leonardo Lima

Hey there! I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Copenhagen under the supervision of Dmitriy Traytel. I am part of the Software, Data, People & Society section of the Department of Computer Science (DIKU). My research interests include proof theory, automated theorem proving and runtime monitoring.

You can contact me at leonardo.alfs@gmail.com and I sometimes blog here.
