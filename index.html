Hi there! I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Copenhagen, under the supervision of Dmitriy Traytel. I am part of the Software, Data, People & Society section of the Department of Computer Science (DIKU). My research interests include proof theory, interactive theorem proving and runtime monitoring. Nowadays I spend most of my time making verdicts of runtime monitors explainable with Explanator2.

Sometimes I write about stuff here.
