Hi! I'm a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Copenhagen, under the supervision of Dmitriy Traytel. I am part of the Software, Data, People & Society section of the Department of Computer Science. I am interested in building reliable systems. Hence, I do (research in) formal methods. I started my research endeavours in the areas of proof theory and interactive theorem proving. Nowadays I spend most of my time doing research in runtime verification. I am particularly interested in the development of explainable runtime monitoring tools.

I am the main developer of WhyMon.
